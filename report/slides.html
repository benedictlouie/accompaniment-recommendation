Stage 2

Chord Inference from Melody

$$P(C_{\leq t} \mid M_{<t})$$

The joint probability of the full chord sequence given the melody. What we ultimately want to maximise.

$$P(C_t \mid M_{<t},\, C_{<t})$$

The per-step prediction. Chord at beat t given all melody and prior chords.
