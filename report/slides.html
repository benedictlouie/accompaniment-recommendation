Beta CDF over threshold candidates. Viterbi smoothing gives a voiced pitch trajectory per frame.
Beat Segmentation
Each beat split into four 16th note slots. Mode pitch per slot suppresses transient errors.

Frames below 0.01 RMS classified as silence before pYIN runs.

Stage 2

Chord Inference from Melody

$$P(C_{\leq t} \mid M_{<t})$$

The joint probability of the full chord sequence given the melody. What we ultimately want to maximise.

$$P(C_t \mid M_{<t},\, C_{<t})$$

The per-step prediction. Chord at beat t given all melody and prior chords. Models this directly.
